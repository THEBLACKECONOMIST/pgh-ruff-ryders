Who We Are

Built by the
Community,

Pittsburgh Ruff Ryders Inc. is a Pittsburgh-based, all-volunteer 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to making a lasting difference in the lives of those who need it most.

We hit the streets day and night — distributing supplies to the homeless, partnering with shelters like Light of Life Rescue Mission, and creating opportunities for youth and families across every neighborhood in Pittsburgh.

Every action we take is powered entirely by volunteers who believe that community is the most powerful force for change.
